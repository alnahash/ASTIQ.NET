About

A studio that makes its own software.

ASTIQ is a small, independent software studio. We build our own apps — products we believe in and want to use ourselves.

Every app is made end to end, in-house: the idea, the design, the engineering, and the release. We're not an agency and we don't take on client work. We just make software we're proud of and put it out into the world.
